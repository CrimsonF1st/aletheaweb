Alethea

.

Mere centuries ago, gods died in the skies above this world. In a titanic war against the unknowably ancient Archenemy, the Celestial gods spent their lives to protect the world from the touch of this eldritch foe. For unknowable centuries they called upon heroes from accross Alethea to join in their fight and, finally, they succeeded - sealing away the Archenemy into the darkest spaces between the stars. This victory did not come without cost. Only ten of the Celestials, who once numbered in the thousands, now watch this world.

Welcome to Alethea

Alethea is a world created for use in roleplaying games - particularly those like Pathfinder 2nd Edition. While there is no discussion of gameplay mechanics on this website, much of the setting has been designed around the features and limitations of such systems.

For more information about the Pf2e system, you can visit Archives of Nethys. The Archives are a freely available and officially approved source of all things Pathfinder.
