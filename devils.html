What Hides in Cathedral Shadows?

The largest region of Reclith is known as Levidia, and it stretches to the snowy regions in the north of the continent, down to the Shimmering Sea in the south, to the borders of the Savrean Coast in the west, and to the edges of the Undying Empire in the east. Perched on the rolling hills, or sitting at the bends in rivers, or clinging close to the edges of forests are the keeps and villages of the Levidian duchies. Each of these duchies is a semi-autonomous territory and is ruled by a duke chosen from amongst the members of the upper class. These dukes are all members of a council called the Rattenauld, the formal governing body of Levidia as a whole, which gathers several times a year. Society in the duchies is heavily stratified; a strict class system is observed on all levels of society.

Real World Influences

Aesthetically, Levidia draws heavily on European gothic architecture - especially cathedrals and other religious symbols. The geography, since Levidia is so large, takes bits from all over Europe. Much of the hilly middle of Levidia is directly inspired by the moorlands of Britain, and I like to imagine that there is a perpetual rain tapping against the lead roofs of Levidian cathedrals. When in doubt, picture the duchies at night, in a thunderstorm.

Notable Locations

Granthrall
By far the largest city in Levidia, Granthrall is considered as well to be the ancient capital of the land. It is peppered with manor houses, religious monuments, and statues of long dead heroes.
Turmvaros
Briefly the capital of a small wizardly empire in ages past. Turmvaros is a city of towers, and is the only part of Levidia where wizards gather in any great number, as most of them serve as advisors and protectors to communities accross the duchies.
Varsés
The largest port city in Levidia. It sits on the southern coast, on the northern edge of the Shimmering Sea, and is the main avenue of maritime trade for the duchies. It is said that there are twice as many canels in Varsés as there are streets.
Kardisten Valley
A mysterious place in the north. It is said that Kardisten Valley is one of the few places where the Archenemy walked upon the earth of Alethea. Few dare to visit it, and fewer still enter into the darkness at its heart.

Culture and Religion

The class dynamics of Levidian society are strictly followed in the cities and centres of religous power, but their pressures are lessened elsewhere. Religious leadership and the ducal families are at the top of the hierchy, with the remainder of the aristocracy just below them. Then are the merchants, the soldiers, then the farmers, and finally the artisans. Anyone who does not fit into one of those groups is automatically below them.

That being said, the wealthist members of society (and those with friends in the right churches) are always able to get away with acting outside their obligations.

Levidia is also the centre of worship for the Ten Divines. Granthrall alone has multiple cathedrals dedicated to each of them, and cities accross Levidia have their own local favourites. Overall, however, they are worshipped together as a pantheon in opposition to the Gods Beneath.
