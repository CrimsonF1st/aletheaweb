A Land of Dragons

On the westernmost side of the continent of Reclith lies the Savrean peninsula, a curving series of mountain valleys and islands that encircles the north-western part of the Shimmering Sea. The Savrean Coast is dotted fourteen great city states ruled by thirteen draconic families. Each of these families is made of only a handful of true dragons who claim titles like queen, emperor, executor and rule with varying levels of authority, but the bulk of the families consist of humanoids - either the result of relationships with the true dragons, or proximity to sheer magical power of living so close to such wonderous creatures.

Real World Influences

Much of the culture, architecture, geography, and even social systems in the Savrean Coast are inspired heavily by the city-states of Ancient Greece. While there is some additional influence from the Italian city-states (especially as they existed in the 15th and 16th centuries.) Therefore, when trying to picture the monuments and walls of the urban centres of the Coast, think of what the ruins of the Greece might look like through a more fantastical lense, and with a touch of the Italian Renaissance.

Notable Locations

Orenia
A city-state on the southern coast of the Savrean peninsula. Ruled by Ayrreiphiath, the Scaled Sun. Known for their strong naval power.
Vasopus
A city-state on the western coast of the Savrean peninsula. Ruled by Rozatheron, the Ashlord. They have historically been the aggressors of numerous wars in the Coast's history.
Epyhra
A city-state on the northern shore of the Gulf of Replidon. Watched over by Asartrastyn, the Mourningdove. Somewhat of oddity on the coast, Epyhra is ruled by an elected council.
The Brimcaster Isles
A mysterious and dangerous region to the south-west of Orenia. These islands have long been thought to be cursed, haunted, and touched by otherworldly horrors. Perhaps this why they are also home to a notorious pirate haven.

Culture and Religion

On the Savrean Coast, your proximity to the dragons is essential. While it isn't the only determining factor in your lifestyle and oppurtunities, those with dragon blood in their family are usually held in higher regard than those without, and most positions of power are held by such individuals.

However, the Coast is also the most diverse region of Reclith. People of all sorts of ancestries end up there, and are often able to do well for themselves in the highly mercantile economies of its city-states.

When it comes to the gods, few of the city-states enforce any religious beliefs on their inhabitants. Most people living on the coast worship a regional varation of the Ten, with some gods taking on more importance depending on the nature of their community. The King in Clouds, The Ashen Fist, and The Arbiter often feature heavily in Savrean pantheons.
